Mapping Olist Brazilian Ecommerce Dependence

A self-directed Power BI case study examining whether Olist’s marketplace growth was broadly distributed or shaped by visible seller and category concentration.

Growth looked healthy at the surface. The diagnostic question was whether that growth was broad-based or quietly dependent on a narrow set of sellers and categories.
