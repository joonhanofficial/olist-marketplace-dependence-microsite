Top-line growth can hide dependency risk.

A marketplace can grow while still being exposed to concentration risk. The analysis tested whether Olist’s growth was broadly distributed or meaningfully dependent on a small set of sellers, categories, or activity patterns.

Category Contribution Signal

9.26% top category contribution

Category leadership existed, but the marketplace did not appear captured by one category alone.

Revenue and orders moved together.

Revenue and order trends rose together over time, suggesting marketplace growth was supported by broader activity rather than only isolated high-value transactions.

The issue was not whether Olist was growing. It was whether growth was concentrated.
